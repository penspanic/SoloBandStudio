MIDI Files - Public Domain
==========================

All MIDI files in this folder are transcriptions of classical
compositions that are in the public domain.

Composers:
- Johann Sebastian Bach (1685-1750)
- Johann Pachelbel (1653-1706)
- Ludwig van Beethoven (1770-1827)
- Claude Debussy (1862-1918)
- Frédéric Chopin (1810-1849)
- Franz Schubert (1797-1828)

The MIDI transcriptions themselves are provided for educational
purposes. You may add your own MIDI files to this folder.
